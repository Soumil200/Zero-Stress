Adho Mukha Svanasana Or Downward-Facing Dog Strech

Technique: Lie down on the floor on your stomach and face downward. Place the palms next to the chest. Exhaling, raise your trunk from the floor. Straighten the arms, move the head inward towards the feet and extend the back, trying to press the heels firmly into the ground keeping the knees straight making an inverted with the body.

Benefits

  • Strengthens the Arms and Shoulders
  • Improves Flexibility
  • Stretches the Hamstrings
  • Enhances Blood Circulation
  • Relieves Back Pain
  • Strengthens Core Muscles
  • Tones and Strengthens Legs
  • Calms the Nervous System
  • Stimulates Digestive Organs
  • Improves Posture
  • Stress Reduction
