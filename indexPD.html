Panic Disorder

Panic disorder is marked by recurrent panic attacks, which are sudden episodes of intense fear or discomfort. These attacks can include symptoms like a racing heart, sweating, trembling, and a feeling of impending doom. People with panic disorder often worry about having future panic attacks.

How To Avoid It

  • Stress Management
  • Regular Exercise
  • Healthy Lifestyle
  • Cognitive-Behavioral Therapy (CBT)
  • Medication
