Surya Namaskar

Step 1: Prayer Pose (Pranamasana) Stand in the prayer pose with your palms together.
Step 2: Raised Hands Pose (Urdhva Hastasana) Raise both of your arms above your head and arch your back while keeping your hands parallel to each other.
Step 3: Standing Forward Fold Pose (Uttanasana) Bend forward and let your hands reach towards your feet while your knees remain straight.
Step 4: Equestrian Pose - Right Leg (Ashwa Sanchalanasana) Stretch your right leg behind you and let the knee touch the ground. Bend your left knee with your arms on either side.
Step 5: Plank Pose (Phalakasana) Step into a plank posit+D27ion where your body forms a straight line with your arms supporting your weight.
Step 6: Salute with Eight Parts/Points (Ashtanga Namaskar) You need to touch the ground with your hands, feet, knees, chest and chin, the eight parts of the body.
Step 7: Cobra Pose (Bhujangasana) Slide your upper body forward and raise your chest in the cobra pose while allowing your back to form an arch.
Step 8: Downward Facing Dog Pose (Adho Mukha Svanasana) Form an inverted with your body by lifting your hips upwards.
Step 9: Equestrian Pose - Left Leg (Ashwa Sanchalanasana) Bring your right knee forward and push your left leg as far back as possible.
Step 10: Standing Forward Fold Pose (Uttanasana) Bend forward and let your hands reach towards your feet while your knees remain straight.
Step 11: Raised Hands Pose (Urdhva Hastasana) Again, raise your arms over your head and arch your spine.
Step 12: Prayer Pose (Pranamasana) Come back to the prayer pose that you started with.
