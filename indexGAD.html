Generalized Anxiety Disorder

This Type of Anxiety Involves excessive worry and fear about everyday situations. People with GAD often feel tense, restless, and have difficulty controlling their worries. These feelings can interfere with daily life.

How To Avoid It

  • Feel under lots of pressure
  • Face big changes in your life
  • We are worried about something
  • Don't have much or any control over the outcome of a situation
  • Have responsibilities that you find overwhelming
